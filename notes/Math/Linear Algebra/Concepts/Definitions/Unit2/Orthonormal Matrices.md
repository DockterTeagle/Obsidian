# Orthonormal Matrices

## Properties


$$
Q = \begin{bmatrix}
q_1 & ...&  q_n\\
\end{bmatrix}
$$

- $ Q^TQ=I $\
  where I is the [[Identity Matrix]]
- Must be a [[Square Matrix|square matrix]]
- $Q^T=Q^{-1}$
