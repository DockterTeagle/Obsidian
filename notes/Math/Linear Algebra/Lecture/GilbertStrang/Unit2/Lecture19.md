# Determinant Formulas and Cofactors

## Continuing from [[Lecture18]],[[Determinants|Determinants]]

## [[Cofactors|Cofactors]]

## Example
$$
\begin{vmatrix}
1&1&0&0\\
1&1&1&0\\
0&1&1&1\\
0&0&1&1
\end{vmatrix}
$$
$\text{det}(A_1) = 1$
$\text{det}(A_2) = 0$
$\text{det}(A_3) = -1$
$\text{det}(A_4) = 1 (A_3) - 1(A_2)$
