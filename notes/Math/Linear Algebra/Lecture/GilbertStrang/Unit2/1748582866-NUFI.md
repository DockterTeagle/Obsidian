# Cofactors

## Definition

A way to connect the [[1748567252-GNCU|determinant]] to ones one smaller

$\text{det}=a_{11}(a_{22}a_{33}-a_{23}a_{32})$
### Putting the '-' sign in

Cofactor of any $a_{ij}$ is $(-1)^{i+j}\text{det}(n-1)$

## Formula

$\text{det}(A)= a_{11}C_{11}+ \dots a_{1n}C_{1n}$
