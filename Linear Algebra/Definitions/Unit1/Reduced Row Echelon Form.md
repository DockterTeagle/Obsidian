# Reduced Row Echelon Form
## Definition
The matrix is in RREF form if every [[Pivot]] has zeroes above and below it, as well as the pivots = 1. It is the most "simple" form of a matrix.
## Example

$$
R= \begin{bmatrix}
1&2&2&2\\
0&0&2&4\\
0&0&0&0\\
\end{bmatrix} \rightarrow \begin{bmatrix}
1&2&0&-2\\
0&0&1&2\\
0&0&0&0\\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
